About Us

SK EVENTS

A PREMIUM EVENT PLANNER

Founded by KK in 2023, and later joined by his friends Kavin and Kaushik, the design duo of SK Events has been setting the scene for weddings, events, and fundraisers etc.
We have been consistently recognized for our ability to bring a customized, tailored planning experience to clients who dream of an approachable, detail-rich, luxury celebration.
We place an emphasis on the fine art of hosting, and focus on the details of a well planned soirée. We love making this journey a personalized one.
We are excited to introduce ourselves, and can’t wait to get to know all about you!
